# 🇺🇸 About Me

I'm **Erick Carrasco Oliveira**, also known as **CARRASCO**.

I am the **CEO & Founder of NOOK**, an edtech focused on building **inclusive educational solutions** designed to improve learning environments for both teachers and students.

My journey connects **technology, education and social innovation**. I started my entrepreneurial path during high school, when I founded my first startup at **17 years old**, driven by the idea of using technology to solve real problems in education.

I hold a degree in **Systems Analysis and Development from Senac** and currently work as a **Software Development Technician**, with experience building web applications and systems focused on educational solutions.

Throughout my journey I have participated in innovation ecosystem events such as **Rec'n'Play**, as well as talks and discussions in institutions like **CIEE**, addressing topics related to **technology, entrepreneurship and social impact**.

**NOOK** was selected for incubation at **i.de.i.a.S Senac incubator**, in partnership with **Porto Digital**, where we continue developing technological solutions focused on **pedagogical innovation and educational inclusion**.

I believe **education should not only train students —  
it should build critical thinking, autonomy and protagonism.**
